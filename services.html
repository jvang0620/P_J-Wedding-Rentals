Our Services


1. Choose Items:

Please take a look at our Shop page and create a cart of the items you want to rent. You will go through the checkout process but you will not actually pay anything on this site. Your cart will be sent to our inbox and we will then contact you about the availability of your items on your event date. We will then send out an invoice and rental contract. The invoice requires a 20% payment to hold your items for your date and the rest (80%) will be due 2 weeks from your event date.

2. Rent:

You can pickup or we can deliver your items the week of your event. We will work with you to find a day and time because we know how hectic wedding week can be.

3. Return:

We will arrange a pickup or drop off time after your event, usually the Sunday or Monday after.
